ATM Cards - CardProblems - Lost - output 0
Report lost or stolen cards immediately to UCBP’s ATM Center Hotline at (02) 811-9292 or through the UCPB Connect Internet Banking Facility “Report a Lost Card” function.
You may also contact your maintaining branch during banking hours (Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM-3:00 PM) to have the lost/stolen blocked to avoid unauthorized use. You will be asked to verify certain information to validate card ownership.
ATM cards that are tagged lost and/or blocked are permanently blocked and will require ATM card replacement subject to card replacement fee of P200.
