How can I view/open my UCPB eStatement file?
You may access your UCPB eStatement through your computer with an Adobe Acrobat Reader (Version 7.0 or higher) or through your mobile phone or tablet.  

For your protection, the attached PDF file is password-protected, which is the date of your birth in this format: DDMonthYYYY.
