Can I transfer funds to other Banks?
Yes, you can transfer funds to other banks through UCPB Connect. Here’s how:
•	Login to UCPB Connect
•	Go to Transfers
•	Click Interbank Fund Transfer

Interbank fund transfers are subject to applicable fees.
