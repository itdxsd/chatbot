What are the procedures and requirements if I will take out the loan from UCPB to another bank?
• UCPB will wait for the Letter of Guaranty from the Bank that will take out the loan, which indicates the loanable amount signed by the borrower and the authorized signatories of the Bank. 

• Annotation of Transferred Title under UCPB 

• Letter from UCPB on the Terms and Conditions for Take Out signed by the borrower and authorized signatories 

• Take out Bank shall settle the outstanding balance in full once the annotation has been done
