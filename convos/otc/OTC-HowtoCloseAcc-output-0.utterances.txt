You may visit any UCPB branch to close your account. Bring two (2) photo-bearing IDs, your ATM card and/or passbook or unused checkbook.

You may withdraw the remaining available balance subject to inter-branch transaction service charge.

Service fee for early closure of account within 30 calendar days from opening date is P500 for Peso Accounts.
